Welcome guys

Welcome to my humble blog created for learning English. I want my blog to be useful, so I decided to share with you my ways of learning a language. So I am going to tell you about the materials, methods and mistakes in language learning. I also write short stories on vocabulary topics. The first step I always try to take is to write the text myself. Words new to me are highlighted in red. After writing the text, I check it with AI. Words and phrases that have been corrected by AI or translators are highlighted in blue. This allows me to see progress in learning. If you are learning English like me, I hope you will discover something new. So don't get bored, let's make our learning more interesting and keep in touch. 🙂
